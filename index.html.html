Our History & Vision

The Holux Twins journey began with a **humble beginning in a simple wooden cabin**. Driven by a clear vision for quality education, word of the school's impact quickly spread. As the student numbers grew, resources were channeled back into development, allowing us to finally build the **standard building of Holux Twins that stands today**.

Ever since, the school has been a hub of activity, participating in **different cultural choreography** within the premises and beyond, celebrating the rich diversity of our community.

We pride ourselves on **high-achieving students**, many of whom love the school enough to still be in close contact years after graduation. About four years back, our students made us proud by participating in the **Pathway Initiative Conference**, fulfilling the important roles of **motivational speakers and debaters**, showcasing the confidence and intellectual capacity we instill.
